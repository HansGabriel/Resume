Hello, I am Hans Daduya

Hello there, my name is Hans Gabriel Beloria Daduya. My parents are Daisy and Butch. I have a younger sibling named Hyle. I am currently a 1st Year Software Engineering Student at Central Philippine University.

I was a student at PAREF-Westbridge School Inc. from Grade 1 until Grade 12. I was a consistent honor student achieving 2nd honors during my Elementary days. But during High School, I decided to improve in my academics and achieved 1st honors ever since. Also, when I was Grade 8, I was offered to join a mathematical summer program. Sadly, I wasn't able to pass it but I studied harder during Grade 9 and joined various Math contest locally and internationally. Some of the notable contests I won were MTAP 1st Place and in the Sinapore Mathematical Olympiad. My interest in math and science also paved way for me to be interested in programming which is why I took BS Software Engineering

The picture above was taken at the Institute of Technical Education Singapore, where the International Mathematical Contest was held. It was my first ever math contest. My facial reaction shows that I wasn't excited nor happy since there's a lot of pressure in these types of contests.

Current Situation

Right now, everything seems fine for me in terms of academics and overall state. At first, I thought entering college would be a bit hard to transition especially during this pandemic since I would be in a new environment and have to attend online classes. But I quickly adapted and easily overcame any difficulties. I would say that I already had an advantage coming to this course since I'm quite advanced in math and have done various programming projects in the past. Indeed it was a breeze for me since I easily aced most programming problems during our class.

Personally, I'm able to this because of motivation and hard work. I try to see what my actions can do in the future and in the same time not getting distracted my accomplishments in the present. It's true that I've done a lot but by being focused on a certain goal, I can achieve anything. Here's a quote that perfectly illustrates my goals.

Work hard in silence, let your success be your noise.

Frank Ocean

Aside from self achievement, I also try to share my knowledge with my classmates and peers. I often do tutoring sessions with no charge to help those in need. Luckily for me, it has also helped me connect with other students who are generous enough to pay me. Anyways, I'm always ready to help especially in academics. Knowing that our course is difficult and many drop it due to its difficulty, I really feel the urge to share my knowledge for others to succeed as well.

For my Future Self

For my future self, always remain grounded and open to new things. Perhaps you're already enjoying life but I hope you remain the same way as I am right now but also removing all the negative habits like laziness. Also, I hope you keep helping others and doing good. Anyways that's all and I'm proud of you.
